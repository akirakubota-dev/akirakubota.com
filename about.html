About

My name is Akira Kubota. I am currently preparing for a career in IT infrastructure and data center operations in Japan.

I began my professional career in manufacturing, working in structured production environments where safety, procedures, and teamwork are essential. This experience helped me develop discipline, attention to detail, and the ability to work reliably in operational settings.

During this time, I became increasingly interested in the systems and infrastructure that support modern technology services. To better understand the IT field, I began studying a range of topics including networking, cloud services, and system operations. Through this process, I found that infrastructure and networking were the areas that interested me most.

I am currently focusing on networking fundamentals at the CCNA level while continuing to learn about data center environments and infrastructure operations.

My goal is to begin my career as a Data Center Technician and build a strong foundation in infrastructure operations. Over time, I hope to deepen my technical expertise and continue growing as a reliable infrastructure professional.
